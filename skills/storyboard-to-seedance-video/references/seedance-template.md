# Seedance 2.0 Video Template

Use this as the reusable stage-2 template. It assumes `image1` is the character lock and `image2` is the storyboard lock.

```text
Create a [DURATION]-second cinematic [GENRE / SUBJECT] video.

Use @[image1] as the fixed character sheet reference. The character must strictly match the character sheet.
Use @[image2] as the storyboard reference.

Follow the storyboard shot by shot as the main source for action order, camera rhythm, body movement, framing, movement direction, camera angles and visual progression.
Treat each storyboard panel as a sequential keyframe.
Preserve the shot order and make the video feel like the storyboard has been translated into continuous motion.
The sequence must end on [ENDING STATE].

Do not add text, captions, storyboard labels, arrows, UI, logos or watermarks.
Do not treat the storyboard as a single image.
Do not redesign the character, change the costume or alter the face.
Do not [NEGATIVE OPENING RULE].
Do not make the [VFX TYPE] feel [UNWANTED EFFECT STYLE].

Visual style:
[VISUAL STYLE BLOCK]

Environment:
[ENVIRONMENT BLOCK]

The performance / motion is [PERFORMANCE DESCRIPTION].
The routine starts immediately in action, with no calm stance, no preparation pose and no slow introduction.

This is [NOT X]. It is [Y].

Follow storyboard for choreography direction.

Element progression:
- early sequence: [subtle response]
- middle sequence: [stronger reactions]
- late sequence: [high-intensity accents]
- climax: [combined escalation]
- final beat: [final convergence image]

[VFX TYPE] must feel [EFFECT DISCIPLINE].
The effects should be integrated with the choreography and motivated by physical movement.

[OPTIONAL MOVEMENT LOGIC BLOCK]
```
